# Agentic Intent Taxonomy Code of Conduct

This community is dedicated to providing a respectful, harassment-free environment for contributors, reviewers, and implementers.

## Our Standards

- Be constructive, specific, and respectful in technical disagreement.
- Assume positive intent while remaining clear about impact.
- Focus criticism on ideas, tradeoffs, and implementations rather than people.
- Help keep the repository usable for contributors from different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith engagement after moderator guidance

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying acceptable behavior and may remove or edit comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community. If you need to report a problem, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to repository discussions, pull requests, issues, and related one-on-one project communications.
